Lunge feint:

Step-over

Pre-fake when possible, plant foot beside the ball as if you are making a pass, swing open foot of striking foot as if you are passing but go over the ball without making contact, land with striking foot in front of ball, next touch should be outside of striking foot to go the opposite direction of the step over, keep ball close, go with speed into and out of the skill move
